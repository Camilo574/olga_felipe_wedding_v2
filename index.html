The Journey

Getting to the lake district is part of the adventure. We recommend flying into Warsaw (WAW), renting a car, and enjoying the 3.5-hour scenic drive north. Alternatively, Olsztyn (SZY) is just an hour away. The roads wind through ancient forests—take your time.

Waking Up

We have reserved Mazurski Raj for our guests. There is nothing quite like waking up to the sound of the lake. We encourage you to book your room here to stay close to the celebration and enjoy breakfast with a view.

To Explore

This is the "Land of a Thousand Lakes". If you have extra time, we highly recommend renting a kayak on the Krutynia River—it's magical. Or simply take a walk in the Piska Forest to breathe in the pine air.
